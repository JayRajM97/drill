Senior Citizen UX Friction

Key usability hurdles identified for users aged 65+ navigating the Swiggy platform.

  • 1

    Complex UI Navigation

    Overwhelming number of choices, dense categorization, and hidden menus lead to cognitive overload and abandonment before checkout.

  • 2

    Small Text & Readability Issues

    Low contrast ratios on item descriptions and impossibly small price tags make parsing menus difficult without reading glasses or screen magnifiers.

  • 3

    Anxiety Over Payment Security

    Distrust of saved digital payment methods. Lack of clear, reassuring language during the OTP process or final transaction confirmation.
