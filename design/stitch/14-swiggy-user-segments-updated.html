Swiggy UX Improvement: Senior Citizens

directions_walk

The Independent Senior

Target

Comfortable with smartphones but frustrated by visual clutter and small tap targets. They value independence and want a straightforward ordering experience without unnecessary upsells.

phonelink_erase

The Tech-Hesitant Senior

Rarely uses apps beyond basic calling/messaging. High cognitive load causes abandonment. Needs extreme hand-holding, prominent voice-search options, and vernacular language support.

diversity_1

The Caretaker-Assisted Senior

Does not use the app directly. A family member or nurse places the order. The UX focus here is on shared accounts, saving specific dietary preferences, and remote order tracking.
