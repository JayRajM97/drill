How would you improve the Swiggy user experience for senior citizens?

Final Recommendation

Solution

To significantly improve the Swiggy experience for senior citizens, the platform must introduce a dedicated "Swiggy Simplified" mode. This mode tackles the primary pain points of cognitive overload, physical dexterity challenges, and trust deficits.

Core Pillars of the Solution:

visibility

High-Contrast, Minimalist UI

Larger typography (Sora/Inter pairs), increased touch targets (min 48x48px), and removal of distracting promotional banners and horizontal scrolling carousels.

record_voice_over

Voice-Assisted Ordering

Integration of prominent voice search and voice-guided checkout, allowing users to build a cart conversationally (e.g., "Order 2 idlis from Adyar Ananda Bhavan").

support_agent

Priority Human Support

A persistent "Call for Help" button connecting directly to a human agent, bypassing complex chatbot flows to build trust and resolve immediate anxiety.

By focusing on Accessibility, Familiarity, and Assurance, Swiggy can capture a growing demographic that currently relies on younger family members for digital transactions.
