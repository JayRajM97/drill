How would you improve the Swiggy user experience for senior citizens?

Before jumping into solutions, pause and ask clarifying questions to narrow the scope.

Key Clarifying Questions

1

Are we focusing on the mobile app, web platform, or both?

Scope constraint: Seniors might prefer web for larger text, but mobile is the primary channel.

2

Is our primary business goal user acquisition, retention, or engagement (order frequency)?

Directs the feature set: Acquisition might mean simpler onboarding; retention means better post-order support.

3

Are we targeting a specific geography (e.g., Tier 1 cities vs Tier 2/3)?

Contextualizes the user: Tech literacy and language preferences vary significantly by region.
